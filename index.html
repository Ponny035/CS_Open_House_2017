KMUTT Night

KMUTT NIGHT is a kinect game with a 3D game with a Thai horror theme using the SIT building for the setting of the map. The story which take place in the faculty, Players can do missions and walkthrough the building to find the way out. So this game used Kinect technology. Kinect will let players control the game with your body gestures. This means that players have to move their body in order to play the game. With this kind of control, we expect to gain more attention from both players and from people passing by. Furthermore, with Kinect, players have to move their body, which is also one way to exercise.


Smart Shopping with Receipt Recognition

People who go shopping without any shopping list usually forget to buy some items that they need to buy and sometimes they do not even know what items they should buy. In addition, they might not get the cheapest price offer from the stores they decide to go. Smart Shopping project is an android application that helps you to manage your household inventory, suggests you smart shopping decisions to get good price product around your area and summarize your shopping history to help you manage your budget easily. In this project, we use optical character recognition(OCR) and the Levenshtein Distance algorithm to read a shopping receipt and add those items into your inventory. To create a shopping list, there are two options which are optimizing price using Set Covering and Greedy algorithms and optimizing trip. We expect users to manage their household inventories and make a better shopping decision with ease.


Refrigerated Truck Temperature and Humidity Warning System

Refrigerated Truck Temperature and Humidity Warning System is a system that detects the temperature and humidity in the refrigerated compartment of trucks. Nowadays, there are a lot of products that are delivered by refrigerated trucks and they need to keep products at a suitable temperature.For example, these are vegetables, fruits, and other fresh products. Because the weather in Thailand is generally hot, to prevent product damage, we need to keep the temperature low in the refrigerated area of food transport until delivery is completed. In the refrigerated area, the temperature and humidity will differ during transport. To be able to prevent product damage, the sensors can alert the driver and can alert the staff at the office, who are responsible for the product. We provide real time monitoring of temperature and humidity by a web-based application and mobile application. We also provide real time location of trucks, and history of transportation on a web-based application.
